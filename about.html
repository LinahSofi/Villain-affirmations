Our Evil Origin Story

Once laughed at in hero school, Dr. Z. Doomington turned to the dark arts of daily self-empowerment. Fueled by rejection and five cups of coffee per day, they created the world’s first affirmation platform designed specifically for villains, masterminds, and anyone who’s sick of being told to “be nice.”

Today, this site reminds evil doers everywhere: you don't have to be liked, you just have to be feared.
